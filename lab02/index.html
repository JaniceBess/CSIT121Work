From first impressions, it seems coding is not difficult. I've also learned that an overload of information at one time is, and has always been, challenging for me. In order for me to learn, I need to master small chunks of information at a time, figure out what that means, then apply it to the next step in a process. With HTML, while I enjoy the success of getting the results I should on a task, I think more practice is necessary. Similar to learning a new language, one cannot be expected to communicate effectively after a couple of interactions or a few experiences speaking the new language.
